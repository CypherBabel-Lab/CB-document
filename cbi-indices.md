# CBI Indices

## Resolutions

CBI indices are available in the following resolutions:

* All
* 1 year (1Y)
* 1 month (1M)
* 1 day (1D)
* 1 hour (1H)

Resolution means the **frequency** at which the data is being updated, as well as the **time window** over which a metric is being aggregated.
